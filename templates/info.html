Welcome to Smarter Groceries! Smarter Groceries is a web application that keeps track of your groceries and when they expire so that you can be sure to use them before they go bad! Over 60 million tons of food is wasted every year in the United States, and we're determined to help reduce that number and pave the way for a more sustainable future. Create an account today to become part of our virtual Smart City and support our mission to prevent waste!
